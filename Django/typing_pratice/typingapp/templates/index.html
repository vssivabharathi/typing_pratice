Easy Mode

Our Easy Mode is perfect for beginners who are just starting their typing journey. It focuses on accuracy and helps build a strong foundation for typing.

  • Accuracy First: Type each word correctly before moving to the next.
  • Error Correction: Practice error-free typing by correcting mistakes as you go.
  • Beginner-Friendly: Ideal for those who want to develop proper typing habits.

Hard Mode

For advanced typists looking to challenge themselves, our Hard Mode provides a more intense practice environment.

  • Timed Sessions: Set your own time limits to test and improve your typing speed.
  • Performance Metrics: Track your words per minute (WPM) and accuracy without having to correct every mistake.
  • Skill Development: Push your boundaries and enhance your typing efficiency.
